Privacy Policy — Calcify

Privacy-first

Last updated: November 2025

1) Introduction

Calcify is a smart calculator app developed by Thejan Vishmitha. This Privacy Policy explains how Calcify handles information. We value simplicity and transparency: Calcify does not collect, store, share, or sell personal data. The app functions entirely on-device and does not connect to external servers.

2) Information We Do Not Collect

Calcify does not:

  • Collect, store, or process personal or sensitive user data.
  • Require an account, login, or identity information.
  • Access contacts, photos, media, files, location, microphone, or camera.
  • Embed analytics SDKs, advertising libraries, or social sign-ins.

3) Local App Data

Calcify may store lightweight preferences (e.g., theme, angle mode, last used mode) locally on your device using standard platform storage (such as Android SharedPreferences). This data:

  • Remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Is removed when you uninstall the app.
  • Is used solely to improve your on-device experience.

4) Connectivity & Third-Party Services

Calcify runs fully offline and does not integrate with third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics, AdMob). No network requests are performed for core functionality.

5) Security

Because Calcify does not transmit personal data, exposure risk is minimized. Your device’s native security controls (screen lock, OS sandboxing, and app permissions) apply to all local data.

6) Children’s Privacy

Calcify is suitable for users of all ages. It contains no ads, tracking, or content inappropriate for minors and does not collect information from children under 13.

7) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect improvements or compliance updates. Any changes will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date. Material changes will be highlighted for clarity.
